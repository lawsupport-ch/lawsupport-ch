## BANKING — REAL CASES

### Banks Used
- UBS: blocked accounts, requires CHF 500,000+ under management for new non-resident clients
- PostFinance: frequently rejects (BK Tesla — "Tesla" name issue; Edel Swiss Health; Aquavit Pharmaceuticals)
- Relio AG (fintech, Zurich): CHF 249/month, fast onboarding, video verification via Intrum. REJECTS US nexus companies
- Payset (UK EMI): video verification via iDenfy, used through Company Creation Centre Ltd (London)
- Ebury: payment service not proper bank account — creates compliance issues

### Rejection Reasons
1. US nexus at UBO/shareholder level (Relio explicit policy)
2. Brand-sensitive company names ("Tesla" triggered PostFinance questions)
3. Insufficient documentation of business model revenue source
4. High-risk jurisdiction of founder
5. Missing proof of substance
6. AML/WorldCheck flags
7. No existing banking relationship

### US Persons
- Practically always rejected by Swiss banks
- Exception: if US person has Swiss residence permit + physically lives in Switzerland + demonstrates tax compliance in both countries
- Relio explicitly: "unable to onboard companies with US nexus at UBO/shareholders level"
- Real case: Engage Anywhere Switzerland AG (US-owned) rejected by Relio, forced to use UK parent company Ebury account → created serious compliance problems

### Key Banking Facts
- Account opening is solely bank decision
- Can be done remotely (no physical presence required)
- Better to have direct address (not c/o) for banking
- Without submitted tax filings, cannot demonstrate "local" status
- PostFinance requires: business plan, CV of directors, employment contracts, transaction descriptions, source of funds
- If company has no own bank account, regulators may view operations as questionable

## SECO PERMITS

### Requirements for Staff Leasing License
- Application form
- Certified Commercial Register extract (not older than 6 months)
- Lease agreement for business premises + floor plan
- General Terms (AGBs)
- Written confirmation: no placement fees charged to jobseekers
- Responsible Person: Swiss resident, relevant qualifications, clean criminal/debt records

### Key Facts
- MYTH BUSTED: "fewer than 10 contracts = no license needed" is FALSE — license required regardless
- Fines: up to CHF 100,000 for provider, CHF 40,000 for client (if client knew provider lacked authorization)
- MSA from US entity does NOT apply to Swiss company — must be issued by Swiss entity
- Payments for Swiss services must go directly to authorized provider
