## MEDIUM findings (open)

### M-1. Static pages have no `dateModified` in schema
- `/` (homepage), `/about`, `/contacts`, `/team/morgan-hartley`, service hubs — WebPage schemas lack `datePublished`/`dateModified`.
- **Impact**: AI engines + Google can't infer freshness on cornerstone pages. Articles are well-covered, hubs are not.
- **Fix**: add `datePublished` + `dateModified` to each static page's WebPage node. Could centralise in Base.astro layout reading a per-page prop.

### M-2. Homepage carries no FAQPage `@id` and the BreadcrumbList has no `@id`
- Sample `https://lawsupport.ch/` BreadcrumbList: `{"@type":"BreadcrumbList","itemListElement":[…]}` — no `@id` or `isPartOf`.
- **Impact**: Schema graph is loose — entities aren't linked. Validators flag this as "disconnected nodes".
- **Fix**: add `@id` URIs (`https://lawsupport.ch/#breadcrumb`, `https://lawsupport.ch/#faqpage`) and `isPartOf` links to the WebPage `@id`.

### M-3. Sitemap exposes 128 URLs but only 1 sitemap file
- `sitemap-index.xml` points to a single `sitemap-0.xml`. With 175+ blog posts likely to grow, segmenting (e.g. `sitemap-blog-1.xml`, `sitemap-pages.xml`) improves crawl scheduling.
- **Impact**: Low for 128 URLs, but worth structuring before crossing the 50k-URL threshold.
- **Fix**: not urgent. Note for future scale.
